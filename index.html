How to Play
  1. Pick one personal box. It moves below and stays sealed.
  2. Open other boxes one by one. You’ll see what they contained.
  3. After each open, the Banker makes an offer based on remaining values.
  4. Choose Deal to take the offer or No Deal to keep playing.
  5. If you never deal, you win whatever is inside your held box.

Early offers are conservative; they get closer to the expected value as boxes run out.
